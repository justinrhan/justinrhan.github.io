Justin R. Han


I'm Justin, a fifth-year dual-degree student at Brown University and Rhode Island School of Design. At the moment, I'm constructing works that recycle motifs from the deep — and not-so-deep — past to speak to Asian American coalition-building, my great-grandfather's work as a Nationalist archaeologist in northwest China, and (always) the narrative and affective capacities of paint and surface.
